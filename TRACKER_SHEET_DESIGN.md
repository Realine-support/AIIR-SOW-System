# Human-Friendly Tracker Sheet Design

## Purpose
Easy-to-read sheet for quick review of engagements. Simple, clear, focused on what reviewers need.

## Columns (14 total - simple and clear)

| Column | Header | Example | Purpose |
|--------|--------|---------|---------|
| A | **Engagement ID** | TECHVISION-20260312 | Unique identifier |
| B | **Date Created** | 2026-03-12 | When created |
| C | **Client** | TechVision Inc. | Company name |
| D | **Coachee** | Michael Chen | Person being coached |
| E | **Title** | CTO | Their role |
| F | **Program** | IGNITE | Tier name |
| G | **Duration** | 6 months | Engagement length |
| H | **Total Price** | $8,112 | Final price |
| I | **Payment Terms** | 50/50 Net 30 | How they pay |
| J | **📊 Calculator** | [Link] | Click to review pricing |
| K | **📄 SOW** | [Link] | Click to review SOW |
| L | **📝 Rationale** | [Link] | Click to see reasoning |
| M | **✅ Status** | Pending Review | Review status |
| N | **💬 Notes** | (blank) | Reviewer comments |

## Status Values
- 🟡 **Pending Review** - Needs review
- 🟢 **Approved** - Ready to send to client
- 🔴 **Rejected** - Needs adjustment
- 🔵 **Sent to Client** - Proposal sent
- ✅ **Closed Won** - Deal won!
- ❌ **Closed Lost** - Deal lost

## Color Coding (for easy scanning)
- Pending Review rows: Yellow highlight
- Approved rows: Green highlight
- Rejected rows: Red highlight

## Benefits
✅ Easy to scan at a glance
✅ All important info visible
✅ Click links to see details
✅ Clear status tracking
✅ Space for reviewer notes
✅ No clutter, just essentials
